Caspar (2023-now)

Caspar is Bingo's son and therefore feels like a grand kid to us👶. He is smart and (just a little bit) cheeky😏 (click on 'Read More' below for his page if curious😜). We are very proud to share that like his father🐕‍🦺, he has been selected as a breeding dog for Vision Australia🏅. You can find photos of his puppies, our great grand puppies 🥰 in his page.

Bingo (2021-2023)

Bingo was such a sweet heart and always eager to learn and help. He did so well at the 12 month assessment🏆 that he was selected to be a breeding dog. He had 27 puppies 🐾 before moving to Adelaide to become a guide dog🐕‍🦺 (Click on 'Read More' below for puppy photos on his page😍!). We miss him a lot but feel blessed that we can care for Caspar, his son❤️!

Kanga (2020-2021)

Kanga was the sweet boy 💝 who went through all the covid lockdown with us. He loves sitting beside the boys when they play the violin 🎻, sometimes even singing along 🎶. What amazes us is how he has different "songs" for different occasions - sometimes we truly feel he's trying to speak human language 🤓.
